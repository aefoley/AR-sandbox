iBright Launch Campaign

Facebook

3 teaser videos were pushed out via Facebook to encourage engagement. Produced record-setting views in 1st month and brought significant traffic to website.

Online and print brochure

Explains the instrument technology, features, benefits and excellent data produced

Email

Sent to existing MyECL customers and new customer base via list acquisition/Google ad strategy. Views tripled previous product launch campaign

Demo shell

In lieu of product development timing, RSK designed and produced a folding prototype of the instrument to help sales people present capabilities to customers in labs around the world. ipad inserted with digital presentation and software interface simulation

System brochure

A 16 page brochure was printed and made available on line to present the entire capability set of the instrument and illustrate remarkable data it can produce

How-to videos

Three how-to videos expanded the story showing techniques for capturing, exporting and analyzing images. Record number of views within weeks of deployment.

Digital animations

Banner ad campaign was developed and placed with ladder animations to engage customers with depth of data obtained on the new instrument. Was also used on email signatures and Facebook pages.

Animated emails upgrade story

Emails with upgrade to new instrument messaging employed “swap” animation that engaged scientists to gain greater capability over the instrument they currently have.
